About

Enable3d is written in TypeScript, and wraps three.js and ammo.js.

Enable3d is designed in a modular way. Means, you can benefit from enable3d in 3 different ways. As a physics extension for three.js, in Standalone Mode or as 3D Extension for Phaser.

There are like 3 levels of wrapping where @enable/ammoPhysics is the lowest level, then @enable3d/threeGraphic and finally @enable3d/phaser on top.
